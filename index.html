About Us

SARUMBO VENTURES LIMITED, is a wholly Kenyan-owned company under Companies Act 486. Its core areas in carrying on the business of water works, building, construction, engineering and renovations with new capitalization and shareholding and also in expansion into buildings roads, housing and waterways construction.

SARUMBO VENTURES LIMITED was set up to provide fit-for-purpose solutions in the building and construction sector, in an efficient, cost effective and safe manner. The company has been involved in a range of projects, mainly in Upper Eastern, Central, and North Eastern regions of Kenya. The company specializes in building of new structures e.g.

  • structural steel
  • masonry
  • reinforced concrete
  • composite structures, and
  • dams as well as the maintenance and refurbishment of existing ones

Gender & Equality


Gender equality and mainstreaming has received significant constitutional attention in Kenya. We are an equal opportunities employer and strive to ensure that there is equal treatment of all our employees. We also strive to ensure adequate remuneration for the services rendered (equal pay for equal job principle). Further, SARUMBO VENTURES LIMITED ensures gender equality is upheld at all levels of its operations, including during the hiring of experts and consultants.
Noting that gender equality is both an important international development and crosscutting priority, we strive to ensure that:

  • Gender sensitivity is observed by all staff as well as consultants selected to make up the roster of local and regional consultants
  • Knowledge of gender equality tools and analytical techniques are availed to all staff and outside consultants
  • Analyze the gender dynamics of specialists in their organizations; this may include reviewing important elements such as roles and tasks
  • Identify gender equality issues. This may include obstacles which different gender groups in the client organization may face in the management of its operations. If there are no constraints, the analysis will demonstrate this
  • Select the gender equality issues that require attention and advise management on the best way to address the same
  • Work with the client management staff to develop a strategy to address the issues raised and overcome constraints
